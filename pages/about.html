About Us

Who We Are

We are a newsletter dedicated to keeping people informed and up to date with the biotechnology industry. Our content is written by actual scientists who are currently working in the life sciences industy and want to impart some wisdom to others. Modern medicine, genetic technology, and artificial intelligence are at a point where they can tremendously transform the way humans live. Dive into any of our articles if you want to learn something new or if you're interested in new and exciting companies.



Mission

We aren't just a newsletter that wants to inform the world about how fun and interesting the biotechnology industry has become. The goal is to also make people want to pursue a career in this amazing field. There is so much that has yet to be discovered and we hope that we can inspire people to join and contribute to our efforts.



Reliability

All our content is written by professionals so our readers know that the information is a reliable source of information. Please share our articles with friends to stop the spread of scientific disinformation. If an article is an opinion piece, then the author will label the story as such so our readers understand the validity of the content.
